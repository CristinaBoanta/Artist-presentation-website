About   The   Band

Disillusioned is a trio originary from Tacoma, WA.
The band recorded their first demo in 2018 and released
the first studio album in 2019. Hard work and talent has
inspired us to achieve a name for ourselves in the music industry.
The band's sound was heavily inspired from the 80s punk rock
and 90s alternative and we sometimes play tribute shows for
the artists who have been an inspiration for our music and
helped us find our destination in the musical journey,
including artists like Pixies, The Smashing
Pumpkins and Nirvana.
